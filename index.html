Welcome to Quills for Thrills™!

Here at Quills for Thrills™, we understand that life can be a lot, and sometimes, you need an escape, or just that extra push when you're trying something new. We've got you covered with our powdered porcupine quills, which are made with the finest quality quills available. With the unique formula we employ to make our product we draw out the little-known, stimulating qualities of porcupine quills that will make you feel like you're in a dream world.



We ethically source our porcupine quills, using only those shed by porcupines living on our local, somewhat-for-profit wildlife reserve.
